Left Content (75%)

This section takes up 75% of the width.

Right Content (25%)

This section takes up 25% of the width.

Explanation
display: flex;: This property makes the .container a flex container, allowing its child elements (the two divs) to be aligned horizontally.
Flex Properties:
.left { flex: 3; }: This means the left section will take up 75% of the space relative to the total flex value (3 parts of 4).
.right { flex: 1; }: This means the right section will take up 25% of the space (1 part of 4).
Background Colors: Different background colors are applied to the left and right sections for visual differentiation.
